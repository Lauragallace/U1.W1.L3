Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie.It wiil become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 cups(500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/3 cups (350g) lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. Youbdon't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
